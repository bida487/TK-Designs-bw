Where It Began

Born from a
Passion for Precision

TK Designs Bw was born from the great lands of Lobatse out of a simple but powerful belief: that every man deserves to feel extraordinary in what he wears. Not just on special occasions but every time he steps into a room.

We started with a needle, thread, and an uncompromising standard of quality. What grew from that was a fashion house that now dresses Botswana's most distinguished gentlemen and elegant women from corporate boardrooms and graduation stages to wedding aisles and gala floors.

We are based in Phase 2, Gaborone and every suit that leaves our studio carries the weight of that origin: made here, made for here, made for you.

"A suit is not just clothing. It is the first sentence of every story you tell."

— TK Designs

What Drives Us

Our Values

Uncompromising Quality

Every stitch is intentional. Every seam is inspected. We do not cut corners because our clients do not accept them.

Client-First Approach

You are not a number. You are a person with a specific body, a specific life, and a specific moment we are dressing you for.

Rooted in Botswana

We dress Botswana men for Botswana occasions. We understand the culture, the climate, and the occasions that define our gentlemen.

Respect for Time

Deadlines are not suggestions. Your wedding, your gala, your graduation — we deliver in time, without compromising a single detail.

Aspirational Aesthetic

We do not aim to meet a standard. We aim to set one — for what premium African menswear looks and feels like.

Honest Craftsmanship

No shortcuts. No hollow promises. Every suit we hand over is something we are genuinely proud to put our name on.

How We Got Here

The Beginning

TK Designs starts with a single client, a single vision, and a commitment to making it perfect. The result: a suit so sharp the client returns three months later with four friends.

Growth
